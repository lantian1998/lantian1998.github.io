Remove OpenVPN's Stable-privacy IPv6 Addresses

On my VPSes, the TAP network interface created by OpenVPN has a randomly-generated IPv6 address with scope stable-privacy.This address itself is created randomly to prevent tracking users by their IPv6 addresses. But when building a network in DN42, BGP handshakes may originate from this address (rather than your manually assigned link-local address), and fail for mismatching origin/IP.The solution is to configure the TAP interfaces with sysctl, and disable the automatic address generation options.
